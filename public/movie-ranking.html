The Best Movies of the Week

My 3 favorite movies to watch at home

  1. Waterworld (1995)

    2. In a post-apocalyptic future where the Earth is covered by water, a mutated mariner teams up with a woman and a young girl to find dry land while evading a ruthless gang of pirates.

  2. On a Wing and a Prayer (2023)

    3. A gripping true story about a man who must bravely land a plane after the pilot faces a medical emergency, using intuition and assistance from air traffic control to protect his family.

  3. Interstellar (2024)

    4. In a desperate bid to save humanity, a team of explorers travels through a wormhole near Saturn to find a new habitable planet, grappling with time dilation, sacrifice, and the enduring power of love across vast distances.
